Delivery

We deliver around Metro Manila. Same-day delivery depends on schedule and distance.

How Delivery Works

  1. You submit an order form from the catalogue page.
  2. We confirm schedule and delivery fee through message/call.
  3. We deliver the camera unit.
  4. After the rental period, you return it or we pick it up.

Fees

  • Delivery Fee: ₱80 - ₱200 (depends on distance)
  • Security Deposit: ₱500 (returned after unit checking)

Fees may change depending on location and special requests.
